About Us

Our story

Jacob's Noodle House was born from a simple dream: to bring the warmth of authentic Japanese street food to the heart of our city.

Founded in 2018, our recipes are inspired by years of travel through the back streets of Tokyo, Osaka, and Fukuoka. Every broth is slow-simmered for 12 hours. Every roll is crafted by hand, every morning.

We are not just a restaurant — we are a place to slow down, connect, and eat well. Welcome to the family.
